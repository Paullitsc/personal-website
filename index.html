About Me

I'm a Computer Engineering student with a strong passion for software development, having started my journey in tech in the fall of 2023. My experience has grown from academic projects and early scripting tasks to full-stack development and hands-on engineering at a big tech company.

I've built systems ranging from data structure–driven game engines to predictive machine learning models, and developed robust APIs designed to handle thousands of requests efficiently.

I'm most confident working with Python, GraphQL, C, and Ruby, but I also enjoy expanding my skill set, recently diving into technologies like CSS and SQL. I care deeply about clean code, performance optimization, and building scalable software that lasts.

Outside of formal roles, I contribute to cloud computing projects and stay involved in the developer community through hackathons and student organizations at McGill University. I'm always looking for the next opportunity to build smarter, cleaner, and more impactful systems.
